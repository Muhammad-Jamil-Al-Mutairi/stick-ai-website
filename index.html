Stick.AI

Proactive Health Monitoring at the Edge

Our Mission

Stick.AI was built to provide safety, independence, and peace of mind for individuals who rely on walking aids. We are achieving this by embedding non-intrusive smart sensors directly into the handle of a standard walking stick.

Instead of requiring users to wear bulky medical alert devices, the stick itself continuously monitors their heart rate and analyzes their walking motion in real-time. If a fall is detected, or if vitals drop, the integrated AI recognizes the anomaly immediately.
